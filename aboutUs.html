About Us

Short Mission Trip is a non-profit and non-governmental organization that operates in the social sector, specifically charity. was founded in 2018 in Bali, under the auspices of the GBI ROCK Lembah Pujian Bali church. Our organization has a motto of mengasihi (love), melayani (serve), memberkati (bless). This motto is in line with our organization's activities, namely helping people in need in remote villages in Bali.

Mission & Goal

Our organization is under the auspices of the GBI ROCK Lembah Pujian Bali church, and our church has a mission, namely to be a Representative of Christ Kingdom. so that our organization also has a mission, namely to be God's representatives wherever we are, which means we must be a blessing and have a good impact/influence wherever we are placed. And the aim of our organization is to become a forum for those who have a desire to help others, and to be a helper for people in need.
